# Punta Cana Ground Control

## Area of Responsibility

Ground Control (MDPC_GND) controls the movement of aircraft on the manoeuvring area south of Runway 09/27. Punta Cana Ramp (MDPC_R_GND) is responsible for the VIP terminal only and once aircraft are ready for taxi they should be transferred to ground control. Once an aircraft reaches Runway 09/27 and requires further taxi they should be transferred to Tower.
