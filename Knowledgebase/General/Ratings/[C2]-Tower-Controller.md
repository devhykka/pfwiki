# Tower Controller (C2)

The Tower Controller rating allows rated controllers to provide local aerodrome services at ?[non-procedural](See the information box below.) airports. 

The Tower Controller rating builds on the foundations of ground and clearance/delivery services, introducing more concepts such as airspace management and [pattern work](https://wiki.pfatc.net/knowledgebase/vfr/pattern-work). Therefore, we recommend that you consolidate your knowledge from the Trainee Controller rating before continuing. 

!!! info Procedural Tower
Procedural towers provide a non-radar approach service to airborne aircraft. Instead of relying on traditional radar or surveillance, controllers utilise pilot reports and more stringent seperation standards. [Learn More](https://wiki.pfatc.net/knowledgebase/general/procedural-service)

To achieve the Tower Controller rating you must: 
- Have completed a sufficient amount of self-study and be knowledgable in all operational aspects.
- Have completed the theory examination with an acceptable result.
