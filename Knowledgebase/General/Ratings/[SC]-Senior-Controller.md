# Senior Controller (SC)

The Senior Controller rating will allow fully rated controllers to connect as any enroute position and provide a variety of services to airborne aircraft. As an enroute controller you will be expected to provide top-down coverage for major aerodromes within your sector. You may also provide top-down coverage for any normally towered aerodromes given local ATC is offline. The Senior Controller rating is highly sought after and is therefore difficult to obtain. It is imperative that you have spent time consolidating your knowledge and ability as an Approach Controller before proceeding. 

Prerequisites
Senior Controller candidates must meet the following criteria to be eligible to obtain the rating:
- Hold a C3 rating
- Be handpicked as a competent C3 controller 

To achieve the Senior Controller rating you must:
- Have completed a sufficient amount of self-study and be knowledgable in all operational aspects
- Have completed the theory examination with an acceptable result
- Pass a practical competency examination
- Complete a month long endorsement where ability is monitored
