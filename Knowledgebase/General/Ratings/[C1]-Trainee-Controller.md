# Trainee Controller (C1)

The Trainee Controller rating allows rated controllers to provide ATC services at any controlled aerodrome. You will be able to connect as a Ground or Clearance Delivery position. To gain the Trainee Controller rating you are expected to have conducted a sufficient amount of self-study and be well versed in the responsibilities you will have as a ground/delivery controller. It is also recommended to observe fully rated controllers to gain an idea of the duties you will perform when connected. Note that PFATC follows the top-down philosophy where controllers connected at a higher position will provide the services expected to be provided by lower positions. 

To achieve the Trainee Controller rating you must:
- Have completed a sufficient amount of self-study and be knowledgable in all operational aspects 
- Have completed the theory examination with an acceptable result
